Professional Profile

Hello! I'm an undergraduate at the University of California Berkeley currently pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Pure Mathematics. I love my combination of fields, and I love sharing the beauties of them to all my students. In my free time I enjoy shooting archery, running marathons, and scarfing down twelve pounds of crawfish!
